# Aukuras
Aukuras houses a perpetually in progress household daemon: Gabija. Appointments, projects, and home systems are tended faithfully here, lest the flames grow unsettled and wander.

---

## what this is

Gabija is imagined in this repo as an agentic assistant who ties together the scattered tools of daily life — task management, personal projects, calendar, and the slow accumulation of smart home devices — into something that runs quietly in the background and keeps things from falling apart.

---

## name

Aukuras is the Lithuanian sacred hearth altar where Gabija, the hearth spirit, is tended. 

She is easily unsettled by neglect.
